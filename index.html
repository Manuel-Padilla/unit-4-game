Crystal Collector

    How to play:

  1. A random number "Target" will be shown at the start of the game.
  2. There are four crystals below.
  3. Click on a crystal and points will be added to "Score".
  4. To win, match "Score" to the "Target".
  5. If "Score" goes over "Target", you lose.
  6. Click on a crystal to view it's value.
  7. Values reset at the start of each game.
